A library of sounds is output via two buttons, a touch sensor, and a switch. The switch controls the size of the circle on the screen, and the two buttons and the touch sensor control three different sounds.

The sound library is designed to be used by two people working together. One person controls a button and touch sensor, and the other person controls a button and knob. This sound library is more suitable as an accompaniment to the performance, and as an aid to the rhythmic drumming.
